Login In and Register process for Explorer App.

How do I sign in with Facebook?

Important tip: Before signing in with Facebook, make sure that your web browser is not signed into someone else’s Explorer App. or Facebook account. Be especially careful of this if you share a computer (such as a public or family computer) – otherwise, you may connect the wrong accounts!

You can connect your Facebook profile to Explorer App. in a few different ways:

  • If you are already signed into TripAdvisor, click the blue Connect button at any time. You should see the button at the top-right of every Explorer App.page.
  • If you are signing up for a new Explorer App. account, click Sign Up in the top corner of any Explorer App.page, and then click the Sign in with Facebook button. You will need to enter your Facebook username and password if you are not signed into Facebook already.
